Vegan Pizza

This delicious pizza that you see isn't the actual pizza for this recipe, but you'll be thankful that your intuition lead you here! This delicious vegan pizza has been years in the making. I don't claim to be any kind of master chef, but I feel I have a solid understanding of trial and error when making this dish. So let us dive into into this dish and get a solid understanding of how to make it!

since this pizza is homemade and deliciously vegan, you will need very minimal ingredients to prepare and cook this dish. If you are somewhat of a beginner, there is no need to fear because you will have this delicious meal cooked in no time. Now let us start with the list of ingredients you will need to start with.

Ingredients Needed For Dough

  1. Any kind of basic dough (Wheat recommended)
  2. Water
  3. Yeast
  4. Oil
  5. Salt
  6. Sugar

Ingredients For Everything Else

  1. Sauce
  2. Cheese
  3. Onion
  4. Pineapple
  5. Jalapeno

Steps In Order

  • Combine the warm water with yeast and sugar. Set for 10 mins.
  • Once set, add oil and salt.
  • Add 4 cups of dough, one cup at a time and combine well.
  • Let rise at least an hour. Can rise for two.
  • Take dough out, cut in two different pieces. Roll both out with Pin.
  • Let proof and rise again for an additional hour.
  • After it had risen, prebake for 10 minutes in a preheated oven at 425.
  • Take dough out. Add sauce, cheese and cooked vegetables.
  • Let cook for about 20-30 mins checking it sparingly.
