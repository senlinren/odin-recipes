Chicken Tofu

Chicken Tofu! Who would have thought? But yes, alas, this recipe does indeed exist! I know you may be somewhat skeptical, but it tastes delicious and is so much easier than you would think. Using some basic tofu, we are able to fully utilize its versatility to truly create something delicious!

This recipe is easy, but does require just a few steps. But I promise you, that you will not be dissapointed in the slightest bit. So let us go on this vegan adventure together and make your vegan chicken dreams come true!

Ingredients

  • Package of Firm Tofu
  • Breadcrumbs of your choice
  • Marinade or sauce
  • A small amount of flour
  • A small bowl of oil

Now let's get into it!

Steps In Order

  1. Press the tofu with something heavy for a few hours at least!
  2. Have the sauce or marinade ready in a bowl
  3. Have a bowl of oil, flour an breadcrumbs on the ready
  4. One tofu is pressed cut into small cubes or irregular shapes.
  5. Take that tofu and let sit in marinade or sauce for a few hours
  6. Now take one piece of tofu, dip into oil, flour, oil then breadcrumbs in that order.
  7. Do this until all are done, place on baking sheet and cook for 40 mins on 425 and flip halfway through.
