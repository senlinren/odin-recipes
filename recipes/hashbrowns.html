Skillet Hashbrowns

Anyone hungry for some breakfast? These scrumptious, cripsy homemade hashbrowns will send you into another flavor dimensions you weren't aware that existed. They are easy to make, melt in your mouth and are so worth making. Best of all, they are 100% vegan!

You can make these with pretty much any pan, but an iron skillet is heavily recommended. This will make them really crispy and give that them "it" factor everyone loves about them With all that said, let us jump into making these puppies!

Ingredients You will Need

  1. Potatoes (Of course!)
  2. Enough oil to continously fry potatoes
  3. Salt and Pepper
  4. Maple Syrup (Trust me on this one, you'll thank me later!)

Steps In Order

  • Parcook the potatoes either in water or microwave
  • Grab iron skillet and poor oil into it
  • Throw parcooked potatoes in oil.
  • Mix around potatoes but not too much. Let them crisp.
  • Cook for about 25-30 mins on medium-high heat.
  • Add spices!
  • Now drizzle them in maple syrup and enjoy!
